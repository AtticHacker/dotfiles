* Notes
** Helpful emacs shortcuts:
*** Navigation   
    M-Space     : One space betweens words
    C-x r Space : Set registry
    C-x r j     : Go to registry
    C-u C-space : Got to last position
    M-s         : iy-go-to-char
    C-M-n/p     : Go to ending or beginning bracket
*** Editing
    C-x r t : Replace rectangle
